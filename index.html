The Ethiopian Orthodox Canon and the Question of Biblical Closure

Most discussions of the biblical canon treat its formation as a largely Western story. The Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church preserves a tradition that predates many of those discussions — and raises questions that Western scholarship has rarely been forced to answer.
